As a UI Designer, you will use Figma and Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD) to design pixel-perfect, responsive web and mobile interfaces, build scalable design systems, create interactive prototypes, and deliver clean, developer-ready assets through an iterative, collaborative process.

What You'll Do

  • Lead end-to-end UI design in Figma, from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes

  • Create polished visual assets in Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, After Effects)

  • Create optimized animations / GIFs using After Effects, Photoshop, and other tools.

  • Collaborate with product managers, developers, and UX researchers to ensure design consistency and usability

  • Maintain and evolve our design system and component library

  • Present design concepts and iterate based on stakeholder feedback

What We're Looking For

  • Bachelor's degree in HCI or any graphic design-related field

  • 2+ years of hands-on UI design experience, with a portfolio showcasing Figma mastery

  • Expert proficiency in Adobe CC (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD)

  • Strong understanding of responsive design, typography, color theory, and accessibility standards

  • Excellent communication skills and ability to articulate design decisions

  • Self-motivated, detail-oriented, and comfortable working in a fast-paced agency environment
